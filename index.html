Dominican Cuisine in Manhattan

Designed by Ismerlyn Gonzalez

As a Dominican-American, food has helped me form a strong connection with my culture. In Manhattan alone, there are over 65 Dominican restaurants. Across these different spots, you'll find anything from mofongo (a dish of garlicky, mashed, and fried green plantains with meat or seafood) to the chimichurri hamburger (a delicious patty on traditional Dominican water-based bread with cabbage and mayo-ketchup). Whether or not you are of Dominican ancestry, everyone deserves to enjoy and explore the array of wonderful flavors this cuisine has to offer.
